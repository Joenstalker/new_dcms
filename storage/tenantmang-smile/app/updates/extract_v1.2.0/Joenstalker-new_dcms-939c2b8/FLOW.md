System Title: 
		Dental Clinic Management System

System Type:
		Multi-Tenant Saas Web Application

Description:
Dental Clinic Management System (DCMS) is a multi-tenant SaaS web application designed to support independent dental clinics in Malaybalay City, Bukidnon by providing online appointments for patients using QR code, treatments, billing, and clinic operations. It offers role-based access for owners, dentists, assistants, and patients, with features dynamically enabled through subscription plans to ensure secure, efficient, and scalable clinic management. It acts as the central provider that hosts and maintains the system.
	

Tenants:
Independent Dental Clinics in Malaybalay City, Bukidnon


Features:
QR Code Online Appointment Booking
Appointment Scheduling & Management
Patient Information Management
Role-Based Access
Billing & POS

Pricing & Subscription Plans

Tenants Responsibilities

Owner (Admin)
Manages clinic information, operating hours, and QR code setup
Creates and manages Dentist and Assistant accounts
Views reports, appointments, and billing summaries
Oversees system settings and clinic operations

Dentist
Views assigned appointments from QR bookings
Accesses patient details and appointment history
Records diagnoses, treatments, and procedures
Updates appointment status (completed, cancelled, rescheduled)

Assistants
Manages appointment schedules and walk-in patients
Registers and updates patient basic information
Assists dentists during consultations
Handles billing, payments, and receipt printing

Patients
Scans the clinic’s QR code to book an appointment
Enters basic personal and appointment information
Views appointment confirmation details
Does not require an account or portal access
